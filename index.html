Welcome to the Clemson Running Club!
Previous Next

The Clemson Running Club is for all levels of runners, competitive and noncompetitive. In the fall, the Club participates in NIRCA cross country races and various road races. In the spring, the Club participates in NIRCA track and field, road races and the Palmetto 200. We have team practices where we run in multiple environments such as the roads around Clemson, the track for workouts, and the trails in the surrounding forests. Other activities include Frisbee Friday, trivia nights, cookouts, road trips, and general team bonding. Everybody is accepted into the club, there are no tryouts, and all speeds are welcomed.

Join Us M-F @ 5 @ Cox Plaza!
